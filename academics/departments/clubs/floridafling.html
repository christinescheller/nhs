Florida Fling 2019-2020

Florida Fling is a graduating senior event. The 2020 event dates are April 22-26. Below is a tentative Itinerary:

  • April 22, PM: Arrive at school to check-in your luggage at the designated time. After all students are checked in buses will leave at approximately 9PM.
  • April 23, AM: Students arrive in Orlando for breakfast. At this time students may change for the day and prepare to spend the day at Universal Studios
  • April 23, PM: Students meet at the designated location at the close of Universal Studios and head to the hotel. Studentâ€™s will check-in and have minimal amount of time to swim and get situated at the hotel before lights out.
  • April 24, AM: Students eat a continental breakfast, board the buses and head to Walt Disney World. Students are given park hoppers and may hop to different parks throughout the day, but must periodically check in with chaperones via text.
  • April 24, PM: Students meet at the designated location at Magic Kingdom to return to the hotel
  • April 25, AM: Students eat a continental breakfast, board the buses and head to Walt Disney World. Students are given park hoppers and may hop to different parks throughout the day, but must periodically check in with chaperones via text.
  • April 25, PM: Students meet at Epcot at close of the park to board buses and return home
  • April 26, AM: Students return home between 6-8am
In order to attend Florida Fling students must meet certain requirements:
  • No SERIOUS Discipline infractions or a history of discipline problems.
  • Passing all CORE classes.
  • Continue to meet all graduation requirements
  • Excessive absences
  • Pay all expenses by the deadline
  • Attend the mandatory parent meeting prior to the trip
In order to reserve a place on this trip:
  • The first payment along with the completed paperwork MUST be received by September 27th, 2019.
  • Instructions on how to complete the paperwork is below.
  • Paperwork and checks go toMs. Miller (rm. 108) or Ms. Speer (Room 904).
  • Any student that turns in their payment or paperwork after this date will be placed on a waiting list.

The cost for Florida Fling this year will be $730.00 per person. This price includes bus transportation, gratuities, and hotel for two nights (4 to a room), tickets to parks, security guards, and the provision of most meals, depending upon rising costs.

Dates to put on your calendar NOW:

Florida Fling can be paid for in one payment or according to the following schedule:

First Payment $146 Due September 27, 2019
Second Payment $146 Due October 25, 2019
Third Payment $146 Due November 15, 2019
Fourth Payment $146 Due December 13, 2019
Fifth Payment $146 Due January 17, 2019
Mandatory Parent Meeting April 8, 2020

Payments:

Payments may be made via My Payments Plus, however this option can only be used to make payments for the amount owed of $146, once per month. If you would like to pay in full or make a double payment, those payments must be made via check or money order and given to Ms. Miller or Ms. Speer. If your first payment is made via My Payments Plus, the student must still bring a registration form to Ms. Miller or Ms. Speer by the due date.

  • All payments made to Ms. Miller or Ms. Speer must be made by check or money order payable to Northshore High School. No cash payments accepted.
  • With the rising cost of fuel, it may be necessary to adjust the cost of the trip. You will be notified if this change becomes necessary.
  • Should a student wish to cancel the trip, he/she will receive a refund of monies paid as long as cancellation takes place before all tickets are purchased.
  • Once tickets have been purchased, a student will not receive a refund unless his/her spot can be sold by the deadline date (February 10, 2020)

Paperwork instructions:
  • Get the registration link from Ms. Miller or Ms. Speer
  • After submitting the online registration form, you will receive an email at the email address you provided in the online form.
  • You must print out the document that was emailed to you, provide the appropriate signatures, and bring the signed form along with your first payment to Ms. Speer by September 27.
