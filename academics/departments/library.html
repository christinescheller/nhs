Library Policies

Check Out Materials

All materials are to be checked out at the circulation counter using a current student id. The librarian will place a stamped date due card in the back of the book as a reminder as to when the book is to be returned. All books are checked out for 10 school days.

Renewal of Materials

To renew materials, each title or item must be returned to the library and the librarian will renew them for an additional 10 school days.

Fines

Each item that is checked out will be charged a fine of $.10 (ten cents) for everyday school day it is late.

Lost or Damaged Boosk

When materials are damaged beyond repair or lost, the patron will be charged for the cost of replacing the materials.

Returning Materials

Library books should be returned at either of the two book drops. These are located at the circulation counter and outside the front entrance of the library.

Copier

A black and white copier for student use is located in the library. Each copy is $.10 (ten cents).

Printing from Library Computers

All computers in the library are linked to a printer located at the circulation counter. Each print is $.10 (ten cents). Color printing is not available at this time. The library accepts cash and a PBS (Positive Behavior Support) Panther Bucks.
